Why This Matters?

Working for long hours? Poor posture can lead to back pain, fatigue, and reduced focus. This system uses your webcam and posture detection to alert you when you slouch or lean.

How It Works:

  • Click “Start Monitoring” to begin.
  • Camera tracks your initial posture.
  • If posture deviates, screen blurs + alert sound plays.
  • Return to correct posture to stop alert.
Your video stays local. No data is uploaded.
